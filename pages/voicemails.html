Voice Mails

Parker Communications is proud to offer Toshiba products exclusively. Toshiba's line of telecommunication products is flexible, powerful, broad, reliable, feature rich, and long lasting.

Toshiba Voice Mail Options: Toshiba pricing is based on model and number of ports. The number of ports determines the maximum number of concurrent conversations the voicemail can hold at one time.

The models are divided into 2 classes. Those with network ability (Strata Messenging, MAS, MicroMAS, ies) and those without network ability (GVMU (CIX28 only), GVPH (CIX40 only), LVMU (CIX100, CIX200, CIX670, CIX1200), IVP8 (disc when gone)).

Older Toshiba model voicemails can be used on current Toshiba phone systems.
These include all Stratagy voicemails including DK, IVP8, FLASH.

New voice mails for older Toshiba DK Phone systems: IVP8 is discontinued when gone. A special version of MicroMAS with analog capability.
