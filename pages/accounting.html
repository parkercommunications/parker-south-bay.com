Call Accounting

Provides statistics about each users' calling habits


Parker Communications offers a multitude of services to enhance the value, flexibility, and effectiveness of your communications system.

Call Accounting lets you:

  • Track statistics and cost estimates on individual extensions, outbound and/or inbound call types as tagged by your employees (account codes)
  • Keep track of the number of calls each of your employees makes or handles.
  • Keep an eye out for abuse
  • Find out who spoke to a particular caller
  • Identify an unkown toll charge on your phone bill.


Also:
  • Each display phone can review their own caller ID history.
  • Toshiba telephone graphical user interface (GUI) will list thats users individual caller history.
  • ACD has a more advanced call accounting reporting package just for the ACD users (see ACD page).
