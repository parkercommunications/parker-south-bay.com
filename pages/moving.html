Moving your Office

Parker Communications can assist in the relocation of your phone/voicemail system:


  • Move your phone service or advise on how to best accomplish this task.
  • Extend your phone service from MPOE* to your systems.
  • Inspect your new location providing maps and/or testing.
  • Supply new or supplemental cabling for voice and data.
  • Audit your Toshiba system and provide upgrade options.
  • Minimize downtime with loaner systems and after-hours work.
  • Plan or build your server room racks and shelving.
  • Our job is to make you look good.


*MPOE = minimum point of entry
