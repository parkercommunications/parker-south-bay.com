Video Conferencing

Reduce offsite meeting costs



Meet face to face with parties from across the globe without packing a suitcase or paying travel costs. Toshiba’s conference call functions can accomodate up to 8 voice conference participants. With the Video Conferencing option, 3 of the 8 parties can be viewed. Video conference participants can be in the same building or across the world on an off-site satellite phone in a fixed or mobile location. Desktop computers and whiteboards can be shared allowing collaboration between parties. Conferences can be held with users on traveling laptops or teleworkers working at home.
